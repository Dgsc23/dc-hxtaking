Practical · Wed 8/5

Final Practical — 100 pts

Take a standardized patient's history, 10 minutes, notes on blank paper. Graded on the checklist below. Dress professionally.

🎯 The practical checklist — memorize this flow

  1. Greet + Chief Complaint, then the HPI with L·O·P·Q·R·S·T (Location, Onset, Prior/Palliative/Provocative/Progression, Quality, Radiation, Severity, Timing)
  2. Past Medical History FAOMASH — Family hx · Allergies · Other illness (chronic conditions / other doctors) · Medications · Accidents · Surgeries · Hospitalizations
  3. Family history + Surgical history
  4. Social History — ask 3+ questions
  5. Sexual History — ask 2+ questions
  6. Review of Systems — screen 3+ systems with a real question each (e.g. “Any GI issues — bloating, burping, hiccupping?”)
